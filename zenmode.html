How to Experience Zen Mode

Before You Begin

  • • Find a quiet, comfortable space
  • • Put your device in Do Not Disturb mode
  • • Sit comfortably with your back straight
  • • Take three deep breaths to center yourself

During the Session

  • • Let each image wash over you naturally
  • • Notice what emotions arise without judgment
  • • Breathe deeply and stay present
  • • Use the space bar to pause if needed

Remember: This is not about thinking or analyzing. It's about feeling, being present, and allowing your consciousness to flow with the visual poetry of human emotion.
