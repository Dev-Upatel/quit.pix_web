Why I Write

These notes aren't polished essays or carefully crafted stories. They're raw moments captured in words, the emotional equivalent of taking a photograph. Sometimes messy, often vulnerable, always honest.

I write because feelings are fleeting, but words can hold them. I write because someone, somewhere, might read these and think "yes, that's exactly how I feel too." I write because in a world that often asks us to be anything but human, this is my small act of rebellion.

"We write to taste life twice, in the moment and in retrospection." - Anaïs Nin
